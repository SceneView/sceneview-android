SceneView Demo is the official companion app for the SceneView Android SDK — the easiest way to add 3D and Augmented Reality to your Android apps using Jetpack Compose.

🌐 3D EXPLORER
Dive into stunning real-time 3D rendering with interactive models:
• Orbit, pinch-to-zoom, and double-tap to interact
• HDR environment lighting (6 environments) for photorealistic PBR materials
• Animated models with physics-based rendering
• Switch between 14 showcase models: toy car, helmet, lamp, mask, animated robot, and more

🎯 AR — AUGMENTED REALITY
Place 3D models in your real world:
• Tap any flat surface to anchor a model
• Drag to move, pinch to scale, twist to rotate
• Depth occlusion and environmental light estimation
• Choose from 6 AR models

🧪 SAMPLES — 14 FEATURE DEMOS
Browse every feature in SceneView with live interactive demos:
• Model Viewer — load and render glTF/GLB models
• Geometry Nodes — procedural cube, sphere, cylinder
• Animations — play, pause, and cycle through model animations
• Dynamic Sky — time-of-day atmospheric scattering
• Lighting — point, directional, spot lights with shadows
• Camera Controls — orbit, pan, zoom
• Post-Processing — bloom, SSAO, FXAA, tone mapping
• Fog — height-based volumetric atmospheric fog
• Text Labels — 3D text with billboard facing
• Line Paths — 3D polylines and Lissajous curves
• Physics — rigid body simulation with bouncing balls
• Image Detection — ARCore image recognition with 3D overlay
• Reflection Probes — local cubemap reflections
• glTF Cameras — switch between multiple camera presets

✨ MATERIAL 3 EXPRESSIVE DESIGN
Built with the latest Material 3 Expressive components:
• Dynamic color theming (Android 12+)
• Spring-based physics animations throughout
• Fully rounded shapes and expressive motion

📦 ALWAYS UP TO DATE
Built-in update support ensures you're always running the latest version.
