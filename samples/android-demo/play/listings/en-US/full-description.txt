3D & AR Explorer lets you discover and interact with stunning 3D models — and bring them into your real world using augmented reality.

🌐 3D EXPLORER
Dive into real-time 3D rendering with an interactive model gallery:
• Orbit, pinch-to-zoom, and double-tap to inspect every detail
• HDR environment lighting for photorealistic materials
• Animated models with physics-based rendering
• 14 high-quality models: toy car, helmet, lamp, mask, animated robot, and more

🎯 AUGMENTED REALITY
Place 3D models in your real world:
• Tap any flat surface to anchor a model
• Drag to move, pinch to scale, twist to rotate
• Depth occlusion blends models naturally with your environment
• Environmental light estimation for realistic shadows

✨ DISCOVER MORE
Explore 14 interactive showcases — dynamic skies, volumetric fog, physics simulations, post-processing effects, image detection, and more. Everything runs live on your device.

• Dynamic color theming (Android 12+)
